Investigation Overview¶

learn about factors affecting trip duration

Dataset Overview¶

Dataset lists the data of 183412 trips made by ford GoBike Bike Sharing app, in February 2019, San Francisco area.

>

(Trips Duration Average in minutes of different Genders)¶

Male riders have the lowest ride average

(Trips Duration Average in minutes of different Subscribers and Nonsubscribers)¶

nonsubscribers take longer trips on average!

(Trips Duration Average in minutes of different Week Days)¶

riders take longer trips on weekends, which is expected for leisure trips, rather than the work-home trips on work days.

(Trips Duration in minutes of different Gender and Age)¶

Although Male rider have higher ride dutations, in range of 60:80 minutes, they had the lowset average among genders.
